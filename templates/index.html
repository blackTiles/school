The schools are category ‘A’ establishment of the Army and are administrated by the Directorate General of Military Training at IHQ of MOD(Army). The Central Governing Council(CGC), headed by the Defence Secretary, Ministry of Defence is the apex body for these schools.


Principal's Message


Lt Col Dipankar Choudhury

(Principal)

I am indeed delighted to associate myself with this memorable occasion of launch of the Platinum Jubilee celebrations of the School. It has been a spectacular journey for RMS, Bengaluru and that I got an opportunity to express the collective appreciation of various institutions and communities concerned to this premier institution.
         I convey my warm felicitations to each one of them as the School has started on its 75 anniversary celebrations. We will continue our efforts in educating our cadets. I remain grateful to all its alumni and the parents for their continued support to the institution and its endeavours and for having taken extra care to ensure that our children make the best use of their time.
